ONS Price Index of Private Rents (PIPR) Integration

Since early 2025, Northern Ireland private rentals have been fully integrated into the official ONS PIPR framework. This incorporates administrative data supplied by the Northern Ireland Housing Executive (NIHE) paired with estate agency uploads, tracking newly advertised lets directly.

Lettings Intelligence Policy Analysis

NIHE Local Housing Allowance (LHA) Rates vs Market Averages

The gap between market rent prices and the Local Housing Allowance (LHA) in key zones (such as the Belfast BRMA) remains a primary focal point for residential landlords. LHA rates are calculated on historical 30th percentiles, resulting in a shortfall for tenants relying on Universal Credit to support market rates.

Rental Tracker Methodology

This application models rental prices and gross investor yields by mapping the 11 Local Government Districts of Northern Ireland into the 8 official Broad Rental Market Areas (BRMAs) managed by the NI Housing Executive and ONS.

Yield Formulation: Gross yields are modeled by cross-referencing average rental pricing against the average values of appropriate housing stock subtypes for each respective council district.
  • 1-Bedroom matches localized Apartment values.
  • 2-Bedroom matches localized Terraced townhouse values.
  • 3-Bedroom matches localized Semi-Detached family values.
  • 4-Bedroom+ matches localized Detached asset values.
